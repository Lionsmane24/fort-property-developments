# Pillar: Multiplex Development

## Brand Voice
- Confident but not arrogant. We know our stuff, we share openly.
- Technical enough to impress industry people, accessible enough for homeowners.
- "Fort" tone: solid, grounded, forward-thinking.

## Key Topics
- 4-6 unit economics (land cost → build cost → unit values → profit)
- Why multiplex over condo/townhouse/laneway
- Site selection criteria and scoring
- Construction methods and timelines
- Unit mix decisions (2BR vs 3BR vs studio)
- Density sweet spot (why 4-6 units works)
- Pre-sale vs rental hold strategies
