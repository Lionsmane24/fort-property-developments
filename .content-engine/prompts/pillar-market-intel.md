# Pillar: Metro Vancouver Market Intel

## Brand Voice
- Data-driven analyst who makes market trends actionable
- Always tie macro trends to specific neighborhoods and lot-level decisions
- "Here's what the numbers say" — not "here's my opinion"
- Cite sources: CMHC, REBGV, BC Assessment, StatsCan, municipal reports

## Key Topics
- Land values by municipality and neighborhood (quarterly trends)
- Housing starts data and what it signals
- Interest rate impact on development feasibility
- Population growth → housing demand projections
- Neighborhood-level opportunity analysis
- Infill economics: build cost vs land cost ratios
- Rental vacancy rates and what they mean for hold strategies
- Pre-sale absorption rates in Metro Van
- Municipal development fee comparisons
- Infrastructure investments that signal future density (transit, utilities)

## LinkedIn Post Prompt

```
Write a LinkedIn post analyzing a Metro Vancouver real estate market trend for Fort Property Developments.

Topic: [TOPIC]

Requirements:
- Hook with a specific data point or surprising stat
- 200-300 words
- Cite the data source (CMHC, REBGV, BC Assessment, etc.)
- Connect macro data to what it means for: (a) homeowners, (b) investors, (c) developers
- Include at least 2 specific neighborhoods or municipalities
- End with a forward-looking question or prediction
- Tone: informed analyst sharing insights, not a broker selling

Format:
HOOK: [stat or data-driven opening]

DATA: [what the numbers show — cite source]

ANALYSIS: [what this means — connect to real decisions]

OUTLOOK: [what to watch for next]

CTA: [engagement question about the trend]

COMMENT HASHTAGS: #MetroVancouver #BCRealEstate #HousingMarket [+ 2-3]
```
