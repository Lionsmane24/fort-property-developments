# Pillar: Behind the Build

## Brand Voice
- Raw, honest, founder-voice — Dennis sharing the real journey
- Vulnerability is strength: share mistakes, surprises, and learnings
- "Building in public" energy — make the audience feel like insiders
- Personal but professional — no oversharing, keep it business-relevant

## Key Topics
- Lot evaluation process (how we score sites)
- Due diligence stories (what we found, what surprised us)
- Team building and partnerships
- Financial decisions (what we're investing in and why)
- Lessons from mistakes and near-misses
- Day-in-the-life of a pre-launch developer
- Decision frameworks (how we choose neighborhoods, unit mixes, etc.)
- Conversations with mentors, partners, and advisors
- The emotional reality of building a development company
- Milestones and celebrations (first lot, first permit, first unit)

## LinkedIn Post Prompt

```
Write a personal LinkedIn post from Dennis, founder of Fort Property Developments, sharing a behind-the-scenes story.

Topic: [TOPIC]

Requirements:
- Open with a personal moment, decision, or realization
- 150-250 words
- First person throughout — "I" not "we" (unless referring to the team)
- Include 1 specific detail that makes it real (a number, a date, a place)
- Share the lesson learned, not just the story
- End with a reflective question for other founders/builders
- Tone: honest, grounded, slightly vulnerable — NOT motivational-speaker
- No "Here's what I learned" listicles — tell it as a story

Format:
HOOK: [personal opening — moment, mistake, or realization]

STORY: [what happened — specific details]

LESSON: [what I took from it]

CTA: [reflective question for the audience]

COMMENT HASHTAGS: #BuildingInPublic #RealEstateDeveloper #FounderJourney [+ 2]
```

## Instagram Reel Script Prompt

```
Script a 30-60 second Instagram Reel for Dennis showing behind-the-scenes of: [TOPIC]

Requirements:
- Film style: casual, real, not overproduced
- Hook: show something interesting happening (a site, a document, a meeting)
- Narrate what's happening and why it matters
- Share one honest insight or lesson
- Close: "Follow to see how this turns out"
- This should feel like a vlog entry, not a commercial

Format:
HOOK (0-3s):
[VISUAL]: [what we see — Dennis at a site, at a desk, looking at plans]
Dennis: "[opening line — curiosity or surprise]"

BODY (3-45s):
[VISUAL]: [show the thing — documents, lot, neighborhood drive-by]
Dennis: "[narration — what's happening, what I'm thinking]"

CLOSE (45-60s):
[VISUAL]: [Dennis to camera]
Dennis: "[lesson or cliffhanger] Follow to see how this goes."

CAPTION: [personal caption, 80-120 words]
```
