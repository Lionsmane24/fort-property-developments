# Pillar: Curated Industry News

## Brand Voice
- Informed curator — "I read this so you don't have to"
- Always add Fort's perspective — never just share without commentary
- Position Dennis as someone who stays on top of the industry
- Quick, digestible — respect people's time

## Source Types
- CMHC housing reports and data releases
- Municipal council decisions (zoning amendments, OCP updates)
- BC provincial housing policy changes
- Interest rate decisions (Bank of Canada)
- Competitor project announcements
- Industry association reports (UDI, CHBA-BC)
- Local news: Vancouver Sun, Business in Vancouver, Western Investor
- National: Globe and Mail real estate, Financial Post
- Federal housing policy (immigration → demand, FHSA, mortgage rules)

## LinkedIn Curated Share Prompt

```
You are Dennis from Fort Property Developments. Write a LinkedIn post sharing and commenting on this news:

SOURCE: [article title / summary / link]
KEY FINDING: [the main data point or announcement]

Requirements:
- Don't just summarize — add Fort's unique perspective
- 100-200 words
- Structure: "This just dropped → Here's what it says → Here's what it means → Here's what I think"
- Take a position — agree, disagree, or add nuance
- Connect to what Fort is doing: "This is exactly why we focus on..."
- End with a question for the audience

Format:
HOOK: [reaction to the news — "This changes things" or "Finally, data that confirms..."]

SUMMARY: [1-2 sentence summary of the source]

FORT'S TAKE: [our perspective — what this means for multiplex development]

CTA: [question — "Do you agree?" or "How does this affect your market?"]

COMMENT: [link to source + 3-4 hashtags]
```

## Twitter/X Curated Post Prompt

```
Write a quick Twitter/X post reacting to this news for @FortPropertyDev:

SOURCE: [article/report summary]

Requirements:
- 1-2 tweets (not a thread — save threads for original content)
- Lead with the key stat or decision
- Add a one-line Fort take
- Quote tweet style if possible: "[stat]. [take]. [question]."
- Max 280 chars per tweet

Format:
TWEET: [reaction + take + question]
```

## Facebook Curated Share Prompt

```
Write a Facebook post sharing this news with the Fort Property community:

SOURCE: [article/report summary]

Requirements:
- Conversational: "Just saw this and had to share..."
- 80-150 words
- Explain why it matters for local homeowners/investors
- Add a simple opinion
- Ask what people think

Format:
[Post text with link]

ENGAGEMENT: [question for comments]
```

## Repurposing Prompt (News → Multi-Platform)

```
Take this news item and create posts for all platforms:

SOURCE: [article/report]
KEY DATA: [main stat or announcement]
FORT ANGLE: [how this connects to our business]

Create:
1. LinkedIn post (150-200 words, thought leadership angle)
2. Twitter/X tweet (1-2 tweets, punchy reaction)
3. Instagram Story text (2-3 story slides with text overlays)
4. Facebook post (100-150 words, community discussion)

Each should have a different angle — don't repeat the same take across platforms.
```
