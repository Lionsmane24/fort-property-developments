# Pillar: BC Zoning & Bylaws Education

## Brand Voice
- Expert educator — simplifying complex municipal rules for regular people
- "I decoded this so you don't have to" energy
- Use specific bylaw references and section numbers when possible (builds credibility)
- Always tie zoning knowledge back to what it means for the reader's property/investment

## Key Topics
- Bill 44 (Small-Scale Multi-Unit Housing) — what it allows, municipality by municipality
- OCP (Official Community Plan) designations and why they matter
- FAR (Floor Area Ratio) — how to calculate, why it determines profit
- Setback rules — front, rear, side yard requirements by municipality
- Parking requirements per unit (varies by city)
- Height restrictions and how they affect unit count
- Lot coverage maximums
- Municipal differences: Burnaby vs Surrey vs North Van vs Coquitlam vs Langley
- Development permit vs building permit process
- Rezoning: when you need it, how long it takes, success rates
